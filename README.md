# Gravitational Wave Simulator

A Python project to simulate a simplified gravitational wave signal produced by merging compact objects such as black holes.

## Overview

Gravitational waves are ripples in spacetime predicted by General Relativity and first directly detected in 2015.

One of the most characteristic signals is the **chirp**, where both frequency and amplitude increase over time as two massive objects spiral toward each other.

## What this project does

- Simulates a gravitational wave chirp signal
- Visualizes how the signal evolves over time
- Provides a simple physical interpretation of the waveform

## Physical idea

As two massive bodies orbit each other:
- they lose energy through gravitational radiation
- their orbit shrinks
- frequency increases
- amplitude grows

This produces the characteristic "chirp" signal.
